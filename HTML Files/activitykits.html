Mandala

For caregivers, teens and children with psychological or behavioral needs

Sending Sunshine Mandala Kits focus on pediatric patients with behavioral health needs. The kit includes self-expression and self-care materials that have been approved by a team of behavioral health experts and has been deemed safe for MOST patients. Please check with your hospital team to decide how this kit may benefit the patients you serve.

Foil Leaf Velvet Mandalas
Pack of 8 crayons
Project Sunshine Journal (no string)
Velvet Sticker Sheet
Project Sunshine activity Brochure
Comes in a reusable bag
